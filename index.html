🚀 New to Star Wars Unlimited? You're Welcome Here.
The NYC SWU community is beginner-friendly and welcoming to all skill levels. Whether you've never shuffled a deck or are a seasoned player, just show up — no experience necessary. Here's what you can expect as a newcomer:
🃏
No cards? No problem. Ask in Discord and a player will likely loan you a deck to play with at your first weekly.
📦
Free cards available. Community members regularly give away common & uncommon cards — just ask in Discord and they'll bring them to the next weekly.
🗺️
Not sure where to go? Drop a message in the Discord and someone will point you to the best event for your neighborhood and schedule.
🤝
Friendly competition. Weeklies are low-stakes and social — people are happy to explain rules, talk through plays, and help you improve.
